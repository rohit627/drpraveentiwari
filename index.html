Dr. Praveen Tiwari

is a well-known face of both electronic and print media. He has vast experience of more than 16 years in electronic media; and is mainly distinguished for his political programs, interviews and public shows. He has been associated with national channels like Live India, Sahara, DD National, DD Bharti and DD sports. His notable shows are “Awakening India” with DD national, “Janta Bole” and “Naya Din Nayee Baat” with Janmat and several other interviews and prime time special shows with Live India.

He has also done notable work in print media. He successfully launched national monthly magazine “Live India” and a national daily newspaper “Prajatantra Live” as a position of editor-in-chief. Dr. Tiwari has also been associated with writing books and he has authored several books so far. “Satya ki Khoj” published by Prabhat Prakashan is his recent and notable creation.

He has done his Ph.D. in Mass Communication from Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalay, Indore. Dr. Tiwari himself is an experienced Cartoonist and he was one of the students of renowned Cartoonist R. K. Laxman in a workshop held in Bhopal.

His deep interest in interacting with students and upcoming journalist led him for many seminars and guest lectures at several esteemed universities and media collages, including International Institute of Mass Media where he took regular lectures of reporting, editing and anchoring.
